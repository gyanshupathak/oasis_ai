🔥 90 Days. Zero Replies. Zero Interviews. Just Silence.

Then I tried something different.

I optimized my resume using 8 simple ChatGPT prompts —
and got 5 interview calls in under a week.

Most people don’t get rejected because they’re not skilled…
They get rejected because their resume doesn’t sell their skills.
Here are the exact prompts I used (save this post 👇)

1️⃣ Spot the Flaws
Prompt:
Act as a recruiter for [industry/role]. Review my resume and point out weak areas, missing metrics, and overused buzzwords. Be brutally honest.
2️⃣ Rewrite for Impact
Rewrite my resume to be more results-driven and achievement-focused for [target role].
3️⃣ ATS Boost
Optimize my resume for ATS using keywords for [specific role]. Keep it natural.
4️⃣ Craft My Hook
Write a 3-line professional summary that grabs attention in 10 seconds.
5️⃣ Upgrade My Experience
Rewrite the experience section with action verbs + measurable impact.
6️⃣ Format Fix
Suggest a clean, ATS-friendly format with strong hierarchy and readability.
7️⃣ Tailor for the Role
Rewrite my resume to match this job description [paste JD].
8️⃣ Standout Cover Letter
Write a compelling, under-200-word cover letter based on the resume + JD.
